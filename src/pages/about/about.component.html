ABOUT US

Trustem Pvt. Ltd. - doing business as Halt-O - is the answer to your parking woes. we offer a variety of parking solutions tailored to meet your specific needs. Our mission is to provide exceptional parking services that make your life easier. "We know that finding a parking spot can be frustrating. That's why we've made it our mission to provide hassle-free parking solutions that are both convenient and affordable.

Our innovative application software provides real-time views to both parking lot owners and users, ensuring efficiency and reliability. But we don't stop there - we offer an integrated system that organizes parking for all vehicles, with a special focus on trucks and lorries on highways. We provide more than just a parking space - we offer value-added amenities such as dormitory services, lockers, free toilet facilities, refreshments, affordable eateries, and 24/7 security services, particularly in areas where robbery, thefts, and other illegal activities are rampant.

Our platform provides transparency and security in parking charges and service delivery, and our services can be easily availed by paying on the go with your phone. Owners can list their parking spaces and earn revenue, while users can search for nearby parking spaces for their vehicles.

At Halt-O, we bridge the gap between the supply and demand in parking, especially in congested commercial and residential areas where parking spaces are inadequate. Trust us to change the concept of parking and organize the market.

Mission

Our mission is to provide a seamless, efficient, and convenient parking experience for both parking lot operators and drivers.

Making parking more accessible by providing real-time information about available parking spots and enabling drivers to reserve and pay for parking in advance.

Maximizing the use of parking resources and reducing traffic congestion by optimizing parking lot layouts and dynamically adjusting parking prices based on supply and demand.

Providing parking lot operators with advanced analytics and reporting tools to help them make data-driven decisions about their parking operations.

Promoting sustainability by encouraging the use of electric and hybrid vehicles and partnering with other sustainable transportation companies.

Enhancing the overall customer experience by providing excellent customer service, using innovative technologies, and leveraging customer feedback to continually improve.

The ultimate goal of Halt-O is to create a more efficient, cost-effective, and sustainable parking infrastructure that benefits both parking lot operators and drivers. By achieving this mission, Halt-O can help reduce traffic congestion, improve air quality, and make transportation more accessible and affordable for everyone.

Vision

The vision of Halt-O is to create a world where parking is no longer a hassle or a source of stress for drivers, and where parking lot operators can maximize the use of their resources while reducing their environmental impact.

Creating a network of interconnected parking lots that are easy to access and navigate, and that provide real-time information about available parking spots.

Developing advanced parking technologies and analytics tools that enable parking lot operators to optimize their operations, reduce their environmental footprint, and maximize their revenue.

Partnering with Government, other transportation and mobility companies to create a seamless and integrated transportation infrastructure that benefits all users.

Promoting sustainability by encouraging the use of electric and hybrid vehicles, investing in green technologies, and using data-driven strategies to reduce the environmental impact of parking operations

Creating a culture of innovation and continuous improvement by investing in research and development, leveraging customer feedback, and cultivating a talented and diverse team of professionals.

Ultimately, the vision of Halt-O is to transform the parking industry into a more efficient, sustainable, and customer-focused ecosystem that benefits all stakeholders. By achieving this vision, Halt-O can help make transportation more accessible, affordable, and convenient for everyone.
